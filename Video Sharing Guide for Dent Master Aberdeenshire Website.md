# Video Sharing Guide for Dent Master Aberdeenshire Website

## How to Share Your PDR Work Videos

Thank you for wanting to showcase your excellent PDR work! Your before-and-after videos will be a powerful addition to the website, demonstrating your expertise and building trust with potential customers.

## Methods to Share Videos with Me

### Option 1: Direct File Upload (Recommended)
1. **Prepare your videos**: Ensure they are in common formats (MP4, MOV, AVI)
2. **File size**: Keep individual videos under 100MB for easier sharing
3. **Upload methods**:
   - Use file sharing services like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer
   - Share the download link with me in our conversation
   - I can then download and integrate them into the website

### Option 2: Cloud Storage Sharing
1. **Upload to Google Drive or Dropbox**
2. **Set sharing permissions** to "Anyone with the link can view"
3. **Share the link** with me in our chat
4. **Include descriptions** of what each video shows (e.g., "Hail damage repair on BMW door panel")

### Option 3: Video Platform Upload
1. **Upload to YouTube** (can be unlisted for privacy)
2. **Upload to Vimeo** 
3. **Share the video URLs** with me
4. I can then download or embed them appropriately

## Video Content Recommendations

### What Makes Great PDR Showcase Videos:

#### Before and After Shots
- **Clear lighting**: Good lighting to show the damage clearly
- **Multiple angles**: Show the dent from different perspectives
- **Close-up details**: Zoom in on the specific damage area
- **Wide shots**: Show the overall vehicle for context

#### During the Repair Process
- **Tool usage**: Show your PDR tools in action
- **Technique demonstration**: Brief clips of your repair technique
- **Progress shots**: Multiple stages of the repair process

#### Final Results
- **Smooth finish**: Show the repaired area with good lighting
- **Comparison shots**: Side-by-side before/after if possible
- **Customer satisfaction**: If customer agrees, brief testimonial

## Technical Specifications

### Recommended Video Settings:
- **Resolution**: 1080p (1920x1080) minimum
- **Format**: MP4 (most compatible)
- **Duration**: 30 seconds to 2 minutes per video
- **Orientation**: Landscape preferred, but vertical is acceptable

### File Naming Convention:
Please name your files descriptively:
- `hail_damage_repair_bmw_door.mp4`
- `dent_removal_audi_bonnet_before_after.mp4`
- `pdr_technique_demonstration.mp4`

## Privacy and Usage Rights

### Important Considerations:
- **Customer consent**: Ensure you have permission to use videos featuring customer vehicles
- **License plates**: Consider blurring license plates for privacy
- **Location privacy**: Avoid showing specific addresses or personal information
- **Usage rights**: By sharing videos, you grant permission to use them on the Dent Master Aberdeenshire website and marketing materials
